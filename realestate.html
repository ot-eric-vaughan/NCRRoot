What Is A Mortgage?

By John Doe

Comments

A mortgage is a loan to finance the purchase of a home — usually the largest single purchase a person will make in his or her lifetime. When you take out a mortgage, you are required to pay back the debt, with interest, over a certain period of time, though the length of time and terms of loans vary. Your home serves as collateral for your loan, which means if you fail to pay off the debt, the lender can take the property.
