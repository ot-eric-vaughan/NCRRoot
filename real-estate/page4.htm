When buying a home, don't be surprised to pay a hefty fee simply for closing a mortgage. Loan origination, underwriting and broker fees, as well as costs for appraising a home or requesting a credit report, can quickly add up. Even though many of a lender's charges are justifiable, others are only there to help increase the lender's profits. Luckily for the homebuyer, some of these so-called “junk fees” are negotiable.
