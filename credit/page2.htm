How Late Payments Affect Your Credit History

Comments

Missing a payment on your credit accounts may not seem like a big deal, but it could severely damage your financial life. If you regularly make on-time payments, your credit score could improve over time. But if you miss a payment (or two or three), this behavior will reflect negatively on your credit history and credit score.

Why Don't I Have A Credit History When I Check at AnnualCreditReport.com?

Comments

Your credit report is established the moment you open a credit account, such as a loan or credit card. If you’ve never borrowed money due to choice or circumstance, a visit to annualcreditreport.com to receive a free copy of your credit report may reveal that you don’t have a credit history.
