Do My Check-Writing Habits Affect My Credit?

Comments

To have a clean credit score, you’ll want to be careful with your checking account. Most banking activities are not reported to the three national credit reporting agencies, so they will not appear on a credit report. Generally speaking, making deposits or withdrawals, writing checks,closing accounts and having multiple accounts are all credit-neutral.
